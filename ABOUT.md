## FAQ

### How do you price your work?
We offer fixed-price sprints (strategy, design) and monthly retainers (build squads). Typical engagements range from $15k-$50k/month depending on team size and scope.

### Do you work with non-technical founders?
Absolutely. We translate technical decisions into plain language and keep you close to the work without overwhelming you with jargon.

### Can you help us hire a team?
Yes. We can help you define roles, write job descriptions, review candidates, and even train your new hires on the codebase we built.

### Do you take equity?
Occasionally, for the right partnership. We're selective and prioritize cash arrangements, but we're open to discussing equity for exceptional founders.

### How long is a typical engagement?
Most projects run 8-16 weeks from discovery to launch. Some founders keep us on retainer for ongoing sprints.

### What if it's not a fit?
If we're not the right team for your project, we'll tell you up front and connect you with alternatives from our network.
